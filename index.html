  • Boy or Girl?
    • I very strongly want a girl, I need my little princess!

    • I am scared of all of the different things that can happen. Will my baby get really sick? Will I do something wrong? What if I don't do parenting good enough? Through good times and bad times, my baby will be my shining star.
